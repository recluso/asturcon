Catedral Metropolitana de la Asunción de la Bienaventurada Virgen María a los cielos, also commonly called the Mexico City Metropolitan Cathedral, is the cathedral church of the Catholic Archdiocese of Mexico. It is situated on top of the former Aztec sacred precinct near the Templo Mayor on the northern side of the Plaza de la Constitución (Zócalo) in the historic centre of Mexico City.
